About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at University of Massachusetts Amherst, supervised by Professor Tongping Liu and Professor Hui Guan. Prior to joining UMass, I got my B.Eng. degree from Southern University of Science and Technology.

My research interests lie in the design of efficient systems for machine learning. Particularly, my current research focuses on the system optimization for large model training.
